  • Is Dark Energy a Cosmic Illusion?

    Mar 7, 2025

    New analysis of the Pantheon+ supernova dataset suggests the "timescape cosmology" model may explain cosmic acceleration better than ΛCDM. Instead of dark energy, timescape attributes the effect to "backreaction" from the universe's inhomogeneous structure. This challenges the standard paradigm and may reshape our understanding of cosmic expansion.

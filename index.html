Observing the Cosmos

I'm Shubhit Sardana

Unraveling the universe's greatest mysteries—from the cosmic symphony of gravitational waves to the precision timing of pulsars.

Research Interests

Exploring the cosmos through gravitational waves and precision pulsar timing

Gravitational Waves

Detecting ripples in spacetime from supermassive black hole binaries using pulsar timing arrays and understanding cosmic mergers.

Pulsar Timing Arrays

Using millisecond pulsars as cosmic clocks to detect nanohertz gravitational waves and probe the gravitational wave background.

Cosmology

Precision cosmology through standard siren techniques, constraining Hubble constant and dark energy using gravitational wave sources.
